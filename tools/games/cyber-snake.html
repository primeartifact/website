Tactical Controls

Steer your neural link smoothly across the synthwave grid.

WASD or Arrows — Steer Snake Direction

Hyper-Thrust Mechanics

Holding Spacebar or holding touch accelerates your snake to hyper-speed:

  • Double Multiplier: Points earned while boosting are instantly doubled!
  • Cyber Barriers: As your length expands, glowing purple grid barriers will spawn to elevate the challenge.
