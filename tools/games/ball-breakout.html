Tactical Controls

Steer your defensive paddle with fluid momentum to keep active spheres inside the matrix.

A / D or ◄ / ► — Steer Paddle
Spacebar or Click — Fire Laser Artillery
Enter / Spacebar — Confirm Modals (Start / Retry)
P — Pause / Resume Game
M — Toggle Audio Synth

Powerup Matrices

Shattering bricks occasionally releases glowing tactical capsules:

  • Multi-Ball (Cyan): Instantly splits active balls into multiple high-speed spheres.
  • Expand (Green): Widens your defensive paddle length by 50% for 20 seconds.
  • Laser Artillery (Red): Mounts twin rapid-fire laser cannons onto your paddle for 15 seconds.
