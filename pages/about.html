What is PrimeArtifact?

PrimeArtifact is a free, browser-based utility platform that brings together Everyday Artifacts under one roof. Whether you need to count words, generate a password, convert colors, or calculate your age — we've got you covered. No signups, no ads, no data collection. Just tools that work.

Built for the everyday tasks you shouldn't need to sign up for — simple, fast, and free.

Our Mission

We believe useful tools should be accessible to everyone. Most utility websites are cluttered with ads, pop-ups, and data-hungry trackers. We built PrimeArtifact to be the opposite — a clean, fast, and private alternative.

How It Works

Every tool on PrimeArtifact is built with vanilla JavaScript and runs entirely in your browser. We don't have a backend server processing your data. When you type text into our Word Counter, for example, the counting happens right on your device — instantly. We use LocalStorage for features like the Online Notepad, which means your notes are saved on your device and nowhere else.

What We Offer

  • Generators — Secure passwords, QR codes
  • Converters — Color picker, number to words, URL encoder/decoder
  • Everyday Artifacts — Age calculator, online notepad, and more coming soon

Who Built This?

PrimeArtifact was created by a small independent team who got tired of bloated, ad-ridden utility websites. We wanted something clean, fast, and genuinely useful — so we built it ourselves.

Open & Transparent

We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't run analytics tools that follow you around the internet. We don't sell data. We don't even have a database. What you see is what you get — a collection of useful tools that respect your time and privacy.
