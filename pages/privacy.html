Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Last updated: April 6, 2026

TL;DR

We don't collect your data. Period. All tools on PrimeArtifact run entirely in your browser. We have no backend servers processing your information, no databases storing your inputs, and no analytics tracking your behavior.

1. Information We Collect

Short Answer: None.

PrimeArtifact does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require user accounts, logins, email addresses, or any form of registration to use our tools.

2. How Our Tools Work

Every tool on PrimeArtifact is built with client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • The text you type into our Word Counter never leaves your browser
  • The passwords we generate are created using your device's cryptographic API
  • Your notepad content is saved to your browser's LocalStorage — not our servers
  • We cannot see, access, or retrieve any data you enter into our tools

3. Cookies

PrimeArtifact uses zero tracking cookies. The only browser storage we use is:

  • LocalStorage — to save your theme preference (light/dark mode) and notepad content. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Inter typeface. Google Fonts may collect anonymized usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • CDN-hosted libraries — such as QR code generation libraries, loaded via jsDelivr CDN. These CDNs may log access for performance and reliability purposes.
  • Cloudflare Pages — our hosting provider. Cloudflare may collect basic web analytics (page views, country) as described in their privacy policy.

5. Advertising

PrimeArtifact does not display any advertisements. We do not partner with any ad networks and do not use any tracking pixels or retargeting scripts.

6. Contact Form

If you choose to contact us via our Contact page, the information you submit (name, email, message) is sent through a third-party form handling service. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with anyone else.

7. Children's Privacy

PrimeArtifact is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

8. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data on our servers, there is no data to breach. Your inputs exist only in your browser's memory during your session (or in LocalStorage for specific features) and are under your complete control.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we'll change the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Any significant changes will be clearly communicated on our homepage.
